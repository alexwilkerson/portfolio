Against all odds you find yourself face-to-face with the portfolio of Alexander Wilkerson. That's me, but most people call me Alex. I hail from the great land of New Orleans, Louisiana. I'm currently attending the University of New Orleans Computer Science Program. While in school, I've found that there hasn't been as much software development as I'd like. So I've decided to supplement my college education with learning how to develop web applications. My goal is to become a competent full-stack web developer. I love to program. When I finish a programming assignment at school I'm always anxious to get on to the next one. So this is where I host all of the little projects I'm making on my way to realizing my goals.

here
is another
little bit about
where I'm
at in
programming

I started programming at age 13. It was all HTML. I figured out how to make text flash and dance on a Geocities website surrounded by vibrant animated GIFs and whimsical MIDI songs. After my initial honeymoon with programming, I took a long hiatus away from the computer. I travelled, made music, worked different jobs, got married, had a kid. After all of that I finally decided to become reacquainted with programming. We met privately and I apologized for my extended absence. I was forgiven. Now I program daily. I know Java, JavaScript, CSS. I know how to use Git. I've dabbled my feet in server-side programming. I've peaked behind the door of cryptography. Oh, and I even made up with HTML, though I've had to leave behind the GIFs and MIDI music. It's impossible to say that I'm good at any of this stuff, but ever since our big break I've been utterly infatuated with everything programming has to offer me. Hopefully I can make this my job one day.
